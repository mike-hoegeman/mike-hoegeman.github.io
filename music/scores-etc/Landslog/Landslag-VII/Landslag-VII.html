Musescore score with tabs

The Score (and tabs) to go along with the diagrams below is available here. The musescore/tabs tuning is 12 string matched reciprocal. It should be easily adaptable to any 12 string free hands tuning. With a 10 string there are 2 or three measures that would need a little extra tweaking.

Notes:

There are 26 (effectively) 16th-note repeated arpeggio patterns in this piece. Most of them are pretty simple and grouped into 3/4/8 bar segments. typically the first one or two notes of each arpeggio is on the bass side before going to the melody side for the rest of the arpeggio.

There are a few that are (very) slightly more difficult.

The tempo is generally around 75 bpm but it varies freely around that.
