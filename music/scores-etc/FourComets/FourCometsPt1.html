Four Comets (1)

This page is a guide to learning the 1st song in a four song suite of miniatures by Ralph Towner called Four Comets on a tapping Instrument such as the Chapman Stick or the acoustic Dragonfly DFA.

This is one of Ralph's shortest and simplest pieces and is a good choice and is much less daunting than most of his compositions. It still has a very nice atmospheric feel (pun intended :) ).

I added a four bar intro & bass section as a (very) minimal harmonic skeleton to go along with the original single voice melody written by Ralph. Feel free omit or alter them. The accel. , ret. & tempo markings are also suggestions, although they do vaguely follow the feel on Ralph's version
