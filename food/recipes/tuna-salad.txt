Tuna Salad

12oz can tuna - drained and fork seperated (bumble bee white albacore is nice)

1-2 Tbsp Diced dried cranberries - microwave in water for 20 sec to soften.
    Salt, Pepper, Onion powder - to taste
1-2 Tbsp minced red onion
1-2 Tbsp Mayo
1   tsp mustard
1   Small spear refrigerated section deli pickle - minced
